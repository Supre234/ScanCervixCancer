About CervixScan

Our Mission

CervixScan is dedicated to improving early detection of cervical cancer through artificial intelligence. Our mission is to make advanced screening tools more accessible to healthcare providers worldwide, especially in areas with limited resources.

The Technology

Our AI model has been trained on thousands of cervical scan images, learning to identify cellular patterns associated with normal, precancerous, and cancerous conditions. The deep learning algorithms can detect subtle patterns that might be missed by traditional screening methods.

Clinical Use

CervixScan is designed as a supportive tool for healthcare professionals. It helps prioritize cases that require closer examination and can serve as a valuable second opinion in the screening process. Our technology is particularly valuable in:

  • Community health centers with limited access to specialists
  • Large-scale screening programs where efficiency is crucial
  • Training settings for healthcare providers learning to identify abnormalities

Cervical Cancer: Facts and Statistics

Global Impact: Cervical cancer is the fourth most common cancer in women worldwide.

Prevention: Up to 93% of cervical cancers are preventable with proper screening and HPV vaccination.

Early Detection: When detected early, the 5-year survival rate for cervical cancer is over 90%.

Screening Gap: More than 50% of women in developing regions lack access to regular screening.
